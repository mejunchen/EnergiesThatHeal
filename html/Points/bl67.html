Bl67 - Jing point Located at the outer cormer of the small toenail. This point boosts the Bladder meridian in its season-winter and disperses it at the end of summer. In all seasons, it can treat the following disorders: Repositions the foetus in its correct position (+), Incontinence (+), Blocked nose (+), Sinusitis (+), Athlete's foot (+), Intestinal parasites (+, during the full moon and new moon), Back pain (+).
